Augmented & Virtual Reality

What is it?


Augmented Reality is reality but with a layer of non-reality around it. An app like Snapchat uses this a lot with the camera of your phone and filter. This way, you can make photo's of yourself (reality) but with filter of a dog around it (augmented reality)

Virtual Reality is an artificial environment that is created with software and presented to the user in such a way that the user suspends belief and accepts it as a real environment, that is not limited to just sight. It can also be applied to other senses.
