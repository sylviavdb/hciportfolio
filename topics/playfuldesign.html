Playful Design

What is it?


This week was about Playful Interaction design. This means that there is a design that can be useful and playful at the same time. Interactive play objects can stimulate social interaction and physical play by providing motivating feedback to playersâ€™ behavior. For example, there is a trashcan that is also a game. These are fun ways to get people throw their trash away or take the stairs.
